San Joaquin (Spanish for "Saint Joachim") is a city in Fresno County, California, United States. The population was 4,001 at the 2010 census, up from 3,270 at the 2000 census. The nearest high school in the area is Tranquillity High School in Tranquillity. San Joaquin is located 11 miles (18 km) southwest of Kerman, at an elevation of 174 feet (53 m). The first post office opened in San Joaquin in 1913. San Joaquin incorporated in 1920. The 2010 United States Census reported that San Joaquin had a population of 4,001.
