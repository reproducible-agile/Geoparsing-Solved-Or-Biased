Melrose is a city in Stearns County, Minnesota, United States. The population was 3,598 at the 2010 census. It is part of the St. Cloud Metropolitan Statistical Area. According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 3.32 square miles (8.60 km2); 3.19 square miles (8.26 km2) is land and 0.13 square miles (0.34 km2) is water. Melrose is located along Interstate 94. Other main routes include Stearns County Road 13 and Main Street.
