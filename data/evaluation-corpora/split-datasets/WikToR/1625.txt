Newport is a town in Herkimer County, New York, United States. The population was 2,302 at the 2010 census. The Town of Newport, located on the western edge of the county, contains the village called Newport. The town is northeast of Utica, New York. The first settlement took place after 1786. The town was formed in 1805 from parts of the Towns of Fairfield, Herkimer, Norway, and Schuyler.
