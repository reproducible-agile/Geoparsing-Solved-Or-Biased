Bangor is a city in Van Buren County in the U.S. state of Michigan. The population was 1,885 at the 2010 census. The city is located in the northeast corner of Bangor Township, but is politically independent. It was first organized as a town in 1854. The Bangor District has an Adult Education building as well. The Bangor Vikings compete in the North Division of the Southwest Athletic Conference for sports.
