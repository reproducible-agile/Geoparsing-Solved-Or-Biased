Clinton is a town in Clinton County, New York, United States. The population was 737 at the 2010 census. The town is located in the northwest corner of the county and is northwest of Plattsburgh. According to the United States Census Bureau, the town of Clinton has a total area of 67.1 square miles (173.8 km2), of which 67.1 square miles (173.7 km2) is land and 0.04 square miles (0.1 km2), or 0.04%, is water. The northern town line is the international border with Quebec in Canada, and the western town line is the border of the town of Chateaugay in Franklin County. U.S. Route 11 is an east-west highway, which intersects New York State Route 189, a north-south highway, south of Churubusco. Churubusco – A hamlet on NY-189, north of US-11, in the central part of the town. Clinton Mills – A hamlet east of Churubusco on Clinton Mills Road. English River – A small stream in the eastern part of Clinton. Frontier – A hamlet on the Canadian border in the northwest part of the town.
