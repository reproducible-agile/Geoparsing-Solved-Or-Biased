Nawa is a city Tehsil Headquarters, Panchayat Samiti and a municipality in Nagaur district in the Indian state of Rajasthan. Nawa is located at 27.02°N 75.0°E﻿ / 27.02; 75.0. It has an average elevation of 369 metres (1210 feet). As of 2001 India census, Nawa had a population of 18,226. Males constitute 53% of the population and females 47%. Nawa has an average literacy rate of 65%, higher than the national average of 59.5%: male literacy is 73%, and female literacy is 56%. In Nawa, 16% of the population is under 6 years of age.
