Polk County is a county located in the U.S. state of Minnesota. The population was at 31,600 at the 2010 census. Its county seat is Crookston, and the largest community is East Grand Forks. The county was formed in 1858 and organized in 1872. Polk County is included in the Grand Forks, ND-MN Metropolitan Statistical Area.
