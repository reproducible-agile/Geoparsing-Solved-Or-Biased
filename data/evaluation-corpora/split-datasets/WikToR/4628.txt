Savannah is a city and county seat of Andrew County, Missouri, United States. The population was 5,057 at the 2010 census. Savannah is part of the St. Joseph, MO–KS Metropolitan Statistical Area. Savannah was founded in 1841 as the intended seat of the new Andrew County. It was originally named Union. Savannah is located at 39°56′28″N 94°49′51″W (39.941066, -94.830873). According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 3.15 square miles (8.16 km2), of which, 3.14 square miles (8.13 km2) is land and 0.01 square miles (0.03 km2) is water.
