Naval is a second class municipality in the province of Biliran, Philippines. It is the capital municipality of Biliran. According to the 2010 census, it has a population of 48,799 people. The territory of Naval comprises a total land area of 10,824 hectares (26,750 acres), which is 19.27% or 1/5 of the total land area of Biliran, making it the largest municipality in the province.
